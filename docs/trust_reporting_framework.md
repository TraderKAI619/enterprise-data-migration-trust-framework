# Trust Reporting Framework

## Objective

Provide a business-friendly summary of migration readiness.

---

## Migration Readiness

Formula:

(Total Records - Manual Review Records)
/
Total Records

---

## Data Quality Score

Formula:

(Total Records
 - Duplicate Records
 - Missing Inventors
 - Unknown Inventors)
/
Total Records

---

## Trust Principles

Migration Success
≠ Data Moved

Migration Success
=
Data Trusted

---

## Business Outcome

Business users should be able to trust migrated records without revalidating every record manually.

The purpose of reporting is to convert technical validation into business confidence.
